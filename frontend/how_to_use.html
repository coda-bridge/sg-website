How it works:

1. For the beneficiaries who have not yet signed up, please click "Sign up" and for the beneficiaries who have already registered, please click "Log in".
2. [Sign up]: A beneficiary can create a new account by clicking the “Sign up” button, please fill in your first name, last name, and phone number. Don’t forget to check the box "I understand" after reading the "Terms of Use". Then click "Get the code". WhatsApp will receive the verification code within 60 seconds. After receiving the verification code, fill it in the relevant positions and click "Sign up". After signing up, the account will be activated. (Note: If you have not received the verification code after 60 seconds, you can click "Resend")
3. [Log in]: Please select the Beneficiary, enter your registered phone number, and click "Get the code". WhatsApp will receive the verification code within 60 seconds. After receiving the verification code, fill it in the relevant location, and click "Log In". (Note: If you have not received the verification code after 60 seconds, you can click "Resend")
4. After logging in, your electronic vouchers will automatically be sent to your account. Click "Pay" to pay with electronic vouchers at the designated merchants. Click "Transaction history" to check out your transaction history of the electronic vouchers. Click the green symbol in the upper right corner to log out.
5. [Pay]: Click "Pay" to pay the electronic vouchers to the merchant. There are two ways for you to pay electronic vouchers.
A) Click "Scan QR Code" to scan the restaurant’s QR code, if the merchant has input an amount of this payment, you will see the merchant brand name and the amount of this payment. All you need to do is click the Next button and process the payment.
B) If the merchant didn’t input the amount you need to pay, you will only see the merchant name but without the amount. Then you need to input the amount by yourself according to your consumption before clicking the Next button.
C) If there is something wrong with your phone camera, you can choose to pay manually by clicking the text non-camera mode, you will be redirected to a new page which includes a drop-down list for choosing a merchant and a text box to enter the amount you need to pay. After this information is settled, click Next to process the payment.
If you paid successfully, you would see the following page showing the details of the payment.
5. [Transaction history]: You can check out your transaction history by clicking on the transaction history button from your home page. The transaction history will be filtered by month. This page will show all the transaction records for the current month. You can see the total income and expenses for the current month. You also can see each transaction’s counterparty, time, and amount. To look into transactions happening in previous months, click on the down arrow to switch.
